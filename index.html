THE PROCESS

How it actually works.

Four steps. One team. No revisions loop with an editor who doesn't get it.

START
01

Scripts & Shot Lists

Our creative strategists send you scripts, hooks, and a shot list — with clear instructions for you or your videographer to film.

02

You Film, We Take It From There

Drop your raw footage into a shared drive. That's the last time you have to think about it.

03

Edit, Caption, QA

Editors cut and caption to your standard. A creative strategist reviews every video for pacing, accuracy, and spelling before it moves forward.

04

We Post It

Once it's approved, we publish straight to your platforms. Ready-to-post content, quality control already built in.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Still curious about something? Bring it up on the call.

What kind of content do you edit?
Mainly short-form social content — Reels, TikToks, and YouTube Shorts — scripted and captioned, ready to post.
How long does a project take?
Depends on your volume and how much footage you send. We'll walk through realistic timelines on your call.
What do I need to send you to get started?
Raw footage, filmed off the scripts and shot list our strategists send you. You drop it into a shared drive and we take it from there.
Do you offer revisions?
Every video is QA'd internally by a creative strategist before it reaches you, so what you get is meant to be ready to post. If something's off, we fix it.
What formats do you deliver in?
Typically vertical 9:16 for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts — captioned to your standard. Other formats on request.
Do you work with businesses outside the US?
Yes — everything runs through scripts, a shared drive, and calls, so location doesn't matter.
How much does it cost?
Depends on your content volume and scope. We'll walk through pricing on your call.
How do I get started?
Book a call above, or send us the details through the form and we'll follow up.
